Curiosity piqued, I approached a friendly-looking student named Lisa, who was one of the workshop participants. "Hey, what's going on here?" I asked, my voice laced with curiosity. The workshop introduced me to various programming languages, from HTML and CSS to JavaScript and Python. I found myself captivated by the logic and problem-solving aspects of coding.
